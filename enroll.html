Enrollment Information

Begin your learning journey at O.B. Montessori Center. Below are the complete requirements, grade levels offered, and step-by-step instructions to guide you through the enrollment process.

Grade Levels Offered:

  • Junior Casa (Pre Kindergarten 1)
  • Junior Advanced Casa (Pre Kindergarten 2)
  • Advanced Casa (Kindergarten)
  • Primary Grade School - Grades 1 to 3
  • Intermediate Grade School - Grades 4 to 6
  • Junior High School — Grades 7 to 10
  • Senior High School - Grades 11 to 12

Enrollment Requiremens:

  • Birth Certificate
  • 2 x 2 ID Picture
  • Report Card / Form 138
  • Primary Grade School - Grades 1 to 3
  • Good Moral Certificate
  • Medical Certificate

Returning Students:

  • Previous Report Card
  • Updated Student Information
